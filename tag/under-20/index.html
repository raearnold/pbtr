6″ Unbreakoball

Posted on June 12, 2013 by Pit Bull Toys
Reply

Time to destroy: still in one piece

The Unbreakoball was created for one purpose: to provide a long-lasting toy that bully breeds love to play with. So, it’s no wonder that ours is still in great shape after eight months. It also comes with a guarantee for replacement if it breaks within 90 days of receipt, in case your dog has titanium jaws.

That said, it doesn’t rank high on our tester’s list of favorite toys—most likely because he can’t destroy it! It does see play time, and it is one we have no reservations about leaving out for free access. But, since it can’t by destroyed and fetch doesn’t hold his attention for long, this isn’t the most popular toy in the house.

Don’t let that deter you, however, especially if your dog loves to fetch and play with balls. This toy is a great value for your money—you should be able to find it for under $20 including shipping. And as Pit Bull owners know, if the toy stays in one piece for longer than a week, that $20 is well spent.

Where to buy

You can buy the Unbreakoball on Amazon, but we encourage you to purchase it straight from the source: Unbreakoball.com, to keep these great supporters of Pit Bulls and other bully breeds profiting.

The Unbreakoball is also available in a 10″ “Big Daddy” size.
