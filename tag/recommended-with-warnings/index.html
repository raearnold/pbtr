Boots & Barkley® Skunk

Posted on April 26, 2013 by Pit Bull Toys
Reply

Time to destroy: 10 minutes*

These small fabric toys are stuffing-free, have two squeakers (head and tail) and some type of crackling material in the four paws. Our tester tears the squeak bladders out of the head and tail within ten minutes of being given the toy.

However, don’t count it as a total loss—our dog is perfectly content to carry the fabric carcass around and lightly chew on it for hours as a comfort toy. He also runs around with the toy hanging out of his mouth and occasionally throws it so that he can retrieve it. You could do worse for $3.99.

If you do buy this, make sure to supervise and recover the squeak bladders before your dog is able to swallow them. Throw it out once it becomes too ratty, or before your dog pulls it apart to the point that it becomes a choking hazard.

Where to buy

This Boots & Barkley® toy can be purchased in Target stores. It’s not available in their online store.
