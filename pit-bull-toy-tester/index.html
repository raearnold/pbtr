Our Toy Tester

Meet Moof (like “Moo” and “Woof” together).

He’s two years old and lives to eat and destroy anything made of paper, wood, fabric, or plastic. He’s mostly-probably full blooded American Pit Bull Terrier (though, as a shelter rescue, no one knows for certain).

When he’s not destroying things that may or may not be meant as dog toys, he spends most of his time trying to be a lap dog or dramatically collapsing onto the ground in protest of an unconscionable lack of attention (in his opinion, anyway).
