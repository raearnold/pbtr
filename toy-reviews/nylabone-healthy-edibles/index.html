Nylabone Healthy Edibles

Posted on March 26, 2013 by Pit Bull Toys

Wolf Size

Time to destroy: < 60 seconds

While these are intended to be edible, we hoped they would last more than thirty seconds apiece. The bone split into pieces in one chomp, making it easy work for our tester to chew up and swallow the entire bone in under a minute.

This treat is not a good choice if you are looking for something edible to distract or entertain your pit bull for any length of time. Find something more substantial, or at least try the larger size.

Buy this instead:

The wolf size is too small for pit bulls, even if they are within the recommended weight range. Try the Nylabone Healthy Edibles Giant size or even larger Nylabone Healthy Edibles Bone Souper instead. Available through those links or in most pet stores across the nation.
