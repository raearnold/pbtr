Courageous Pit Bulls in the News

Posted on May 19, 2013 by Pit Bull Toys

In the mass of news articles that (often erroneously) blame bully breeds for any number of attacks and brutal actions are media reports on pit bulls at their best: performing loving, loyal, and courageous acts:

  • A Boston pit bull, Lilly, saved her owner from an oncoming train.
  • A family pit bull in Georgia alerted one owner that his wife had fallen. Had the dog not let him know something was wrong, there would have been no time to save her.
  • A stray pit bull in Port Charlotte, FL saved a woman and her young son from a knife-wielding attacker.
  • A stray pit bull puppy in Ontario protected a woman from cars as she had a stroke while walking on the side of the road, and alerted her husband of the issue. Because of Ontario laws, the dog almost lost its life purely for being a bully breed, but thankfully they were able to send “Faith” to a caring home in B.C.

What is your favorite feel-good bully breed story in the news?
